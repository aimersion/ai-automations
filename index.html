How it works

Every tool runs 100% in your browser. Your emails and calendar data never touch our servers — or anyone else's.

1

Get a free API key

Grab a free Gemini key from Google AI Studio. No credit card. Takes 60 seconds. Paste it once — it saves in your browser.

2

Connect your accounts

Use the same secure OAuth login flow as Zapier or HubSpot. We never see your credentials or store your data.

3

Let AI do the work

Everything runs locally in your tab. Close it and it's gone — nothing is persisted on any server. Open source on GitHub.
